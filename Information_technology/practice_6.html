Future possibilities after completing this course

  • Some of the colleges of DU do provide on campus placements to Computer science hons students, but its very less in number so you have to work hard to grab it or you have to explore the outside world and not to rely on your college.
  • Some of the Tech companies hire students from DU for technical support system and consultancy.
  • After completing B.Sc in Computer science, one can always go for higher studies for a better career prospects. They can join Master of Computer Application (MCA) of M.Sc in Computer science course.
  • There are lot of fields in which a DU B.sc hons in CS candidate make his/her career according to the interest.
